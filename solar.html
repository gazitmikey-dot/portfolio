Solar Analytic
App

Solar provides high-accuracy, serverless analytics for monitoring and optimizing solar PV performance. Their technology enables energy platforms and operators to assess system health, automate maintenance, and reduce costs — without the need for expensive weather sensors. Backed by the Israel Innovation Authority, Solnet supports smarter, decentralized energy management across solar, storage, and EV systems.

My Role & Contributions

This project was created as part of a studio, where I led the product design and was responsible for the project end-to-end. A key challenge was designing a fleet management experience that supports a large number of systems, each with multiple data points, while maintaining clarity, hierarchy, and usability at scale.

Fleet Management

Designed a scalable fleet management view that adapts to the number of systems — from small residential setups to large commercial installations. Each tier presents the right level of detail and hierarchy without overwhelming the user.

Analytics & Reporting

Built a comprehensive analytics layer that lets operators track performance across multiple time horizons — monthly, yearly, and total lifetime — with detailed breakdowns per system, enabling fast identification of underperforming units.

System Features

Designed supporting features including subscription management, system alerts, user profiles, and a contact flow — ensuring the platform covers the full operational lifecycle of managing a solar installation.

Conclusion

This project pushed me to solve complex data-density challenges while keeping the experience clean and usable. Designing for scale — across fleet sizes, time periods, and user types — strengthened my ability to build flexible, systematic design solutions that hold up under real-world complexity.
