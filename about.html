About me

Hi, I'm Michal Gazit Shprung, a UX/UI product designer who turns complex platforms into intuitive experiences. I'm a mother of three based in Beit Yehoshua, Israel.

With over 7 years of design experience and a background in law, I specialize in simplifying complex workflows across sophisticated platforms — including compliance tools, cloud security monitoring solutions, and AI-powered compliance questionnaires. As the Product UX/UI Lead at Findings, I design end-to-end experiences that bring clarity, structure, and usability to complex enterprise products.

I lead projects from research through development, working closely with product, engineering, and customer success teams to ensure solutions are practical, scalable, and aligned with real user needs.

I use AI as a design accelerator — to test ideas faster, reduce friction between design and development, and sharpen product decisions early.

I have a deep love for visual art and design, which consistently inspires my creative approach. I'm always looking to grow — and would love to hear from you.
